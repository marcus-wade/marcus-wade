<summary>📖 Full DJ bio</summary>

<br>

Since the mid 90's, DJ Marcus has been spinning house music in the US. In 1998, he renovated and opened Club Salt Shaker in North Carolina. This nightclub was the first of it's kind as it mainly focused on purely inspirational house music. In 2001, DJ Marcus created and launched the independently syndicated radio program "Cross Culture Radio", a 2 hour radio show that featured the latest in house music seamlessly mixed on several terrestrial radio stations. Today, Cross Culture Radio has been rebranded as Gospel Meets Dance Radio and is currently broadcasted on radio in Amsterdam via CLFrequency and over various internet radio stations in Africa and Colombia. Salt Shaker Records launched in early 2010, focusing on the creation and promotion of quality house music and events.

In 2018, DJ Marcus joined the artist roster of PTGW Music and IndiePlant Gospel managed by the Gospel Agency of Amsterdam, NL with the main focus of expansion of the DJ Marcus brand in the Netherlands and throughout Europe. DJ Marcus was officially introduced to Europe during ESNS 2019 Showcase Festival teaching masterclasses and performing during DanceFair 2019 and ADE 2019 all with the ultimate goal of introducing gospel and inspirational dance music into the European markets.

The future is bright for DJ Marcus as he continues to expand his musical brand internationally and fulfill his creed to reach this at-risk generation through inspirational dance music.
